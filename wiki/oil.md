# Oil

## Smoking Point

## List of neutral oils

Neutral oils are those with little noticeable flavours. They include
* Avocado oil
* Canola oil
* Corn oil
* Grapeseed oil
* Palm oil
* Rice bran oil
* Safflower oil
* Soybean oil
* Sunflower oil
* Vegetable oil
* Refined coconut oil

In small quantities, the following can also be considered neutral:
* Peanut oil
* Light, refined, or non-virgin olive oil

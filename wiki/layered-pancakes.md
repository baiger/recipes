# Layered Pancakes

## Ingredients
* Dough
   * Flour 100%
   * Water 67%
* For each 8" pancake
   * Dough 55g
   * Oil-rich filling 2 tbsps, or for traditional scallion oil pancakes
      * Salt 1.5g
      * Neutral oil 7g
      * Green onion, minced, 2 tbsps

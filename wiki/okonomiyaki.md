# Okonomiyaki

This recipe references and simplifies <https://www.justonecookbook.com/okonomiyaki/>.

## Sauce Substitutes

### "Kewpie" Mayo
* 3 parts Mayonnaise
* 1 part (Dijon) mustard

### Okonomiyaki Sauce
* 4 parts ketchup
* 3 parts Worcestershire sauce
* 2 parts oyster sauce
* 1 part sugar
